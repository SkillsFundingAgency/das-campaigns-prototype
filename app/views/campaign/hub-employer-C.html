HOW TO HIRE AN APPRENTICE

With the help of our online tools, you’ll find the right apprenticeship and training provider to enhance your business – and of course,
a future apprentice who’s bursting with fresh ideas.

Here are our key steps to success.

EXPLORE YOUR OPTIONS

Still deciding whether apprenticeships are right for your business? Take a look around – we’ve gathered plenty of valuable hints and tips to help you make your decision.

SHAPE YOUR APPRENTICESHIP

When you’re ready to get started, you can use our online tool to find the right apprenticeship for your business and team up with your favourite training provider.

CREATE A VACANCY

Working with your training provider, you’ll create a vacancy for the new apprenticeship. This should set out all the important details, helping applicants work out whether it’s a good match for them.

GET IT APPROVED

Before your vacancy is advertised, it needs to be approved by the National Contact Centre (NCC).

helpdesk@manage-apprenticeships.service.gov.uk
or give them a call on 08000 150 600

SPREAD THE WORD

Once your vacancy has been approved, it will be advertised here for free, ready for potential apprentices to apply.

CHOOSE YOUR APPRENTICE

Now for the exciting bit – it’s time to choose your apprentice. You’ll receive applications from your training provider, and together you’ll decide who’s the right fit.

FIRE IT UP

Congratulations – you’ve hired an apprentice! The successful applicant will be added to your account, and you’ll soon experience the energy, talent and enthusiasm an apprentice brings.
