DEVELOPING FUTURE LEADERS

Apprenticeships form part of our talent pipeline, from which we identify future leaders. In our experience, apprentices are hungry to learn and develop new skills. They bring a fresh perspective, commitment to our values, and high rates of retention. They help us to develop our workforce with the skills that we need as a business, now and in the future.

WORKING WITH THE RIGHT TRAINING PROVIDER

Our apprentices’ experience both on and off the job is fundamental to the success of the programme and their future development. We work closely with our training providers in supporting our apprentices to gain a breadth of experience that’s relevant to their study.

LEVELING THE PLAYING FIELD

In 2015 we removed academic entry criteria from our student recruitment process, broadening our pool of talent. We also operate a ‘blind CV’ policy that helps us to create a level playing field for all candidates and promotes greater diversity among our workforce.
