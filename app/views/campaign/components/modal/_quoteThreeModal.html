CHRISTIE’S

A BRIGHT, ENGAGED AND DIVERSE WORKFORCE

Our Apprenticeship Programme provides us with a talent pipeline for the future, and our experience so far has been overwhelmingly positive. We have found bright, engaged young people who have made a choice to not attend university. It has also been a great channel for us to start increasing diversity in our business.

TEAMING UP WITH TRAINING PROVIDERS

We put a lot of time and effort into selecting our training providers – it’s so important for the apprentice’s overall experience. We want to work with partners who are invested in the apprentices and want to be hands on and ‘high touch’ with us as the employer. We want socially conscious business partners.

FINDING THE RIGHT APPRENTICE

To find an apprentice, we use a multi-pronged approach of direct outreach to schools, coupled with working with external providers such as White Hat and ThePrince’s Trust. When it comes to seeking help with the process, we’ve found people in our network to be incredibly helpful and willing to share their experiences and best practice.
