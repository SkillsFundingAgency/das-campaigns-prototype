Welcome to the Careers advice hub Apprenticeships

The Careers advice hub is home to the Apprenticeship Support & Knowledge for schools and colleges (ASK) programme. This is your go-to place to access resources, become an Apprenticeship Champion or request support to help you spread the apprenticeship message.

    Free support

    Request free support for your school or establishment

    We’ll connect your school, college or educational establishment with an ASK delivery partner in your area. They’ll work with you to deliver apprenticeship support based on your needs, from visits and talks to resources and specific event support. Fill in our quick online form to get started.

    Access free online resources

    Take a look through our free online resources to learn more about apprenticeships. They’ll help you navigate the world of apprenticeships, gaining confidence to inform and empower future apprentices.

    Broadcasts

    View live broadcasts - or watch them on catch-up

    Our live broadcasts are designed to give schools and establishments across England the very latest apprenticeship information. Meet inspiring apprentices and hear from the employers who are helping to shape their careers. There’s a catch-up service too, so you can watch them at any time.

    Become an Apprenticeship Champion

    Are you keen to promote apprenticeships in your school or establishment? Let us support you in your role as an Apprenticeship Champion. It’s quick, easy and free to sign up, and you’ll get access to inspirational guidance and free resources to help you with your goals.
