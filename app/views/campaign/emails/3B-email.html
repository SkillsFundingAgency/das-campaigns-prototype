If you’re interested in taking on an apprentice, you may have wondered who’s eligible.

  1. Apprenticeships are available at various levels. They can be equivalent to GCSE level, or even a master’s degree.
  2. Your apprentice can be any age, as long as they’re 16 or over by the end of the summer holidays.
  3. Apprenticeships are great for hiring new staff, but they’re also ideal for upskilling or retraining your current staff, so you can fill skill gaps, boost motivation and improve retention.
