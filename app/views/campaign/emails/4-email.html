How to fund an apprenticeship

Hi <name>,

Here are the key things you need to know about funding an apprenticeship.

  1. As an employer with a pay bill of less than £3 million per year, you’ll get funding from the government to pay towards training and assessing apprentices.
  2. The amount of funding you get depends on the number of employees you have and the apprentice you’re hiring.
  3. You’ll need to pay the apprentice’s salary, which will be at least the hourly minimum wage rate for their circumstances. Currently, this is £4.15 for apprentices who are aged under 19, or aged 19 or over and in the first year of their apprenticeship.
