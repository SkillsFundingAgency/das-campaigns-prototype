Accessing government funding

Hi <name>,

The way apprenticeship funding works depends on whether your organisation pays the apprenticeship levy (which is based on your annual pay bill - the amount you spend on things like wages, bonuses and commissions).

If you’re an employer with a pay bill of less than £3 million per year

You’ll get funding from the government to pay towards training and assessing apprentices. The amount of funding you get depends on the number of employees you have and the apprentice you’re hiring.

If you’re an employer with a pay bill of more than £3 million per year

You’re required to pay the apprenticeship levy. You’ll receive funds through the apprenticeship service to spend on training and assessing your apprentices.
