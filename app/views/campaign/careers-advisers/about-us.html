APPRENTICESHIP SUPPORT & KNOWLEDGE FOR SCHOOLS AND COLLEGES

The Apprenticeship Support and Knowledge for schools and colleges programme (ASK) is funded by the National Apprenticeship Service, part of the Department for Education. The programme is designed to give schools and other establishments in England free support to transform how your students think about apprenticeships.

What that support looks like for you will depend on your establishment’s size, focus and requirements. There are a whole range of options, including:

  • inspiring apprenticeship awareness assemblies
  • apprenticeship application workshops
  • careers fair attendance
  • free resources
  • teacher CPD sessions

Fill in our quick online form and we’ll connect you with your local ASK delivery partner organisation. They’ll work with you to deliver a support package that’s tailored to your needs.
